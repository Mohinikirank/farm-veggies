STRAWBERRY

Extremely juicy and syrupy, these conical heart shaped fruits have seeds on the skin that give them a unique texture. With a blend of sweet-tart flavour, these are everyone's favourite berries

BROCCOLI

Consume fresh broccoli as soon as you can as it will not keep for long,With a shape resembling that of a cauliflower, Brocollis have clusters of small, tight flower heads. These heads turn bright green on cooking and tastes slightly bitter.

CARROT

They have thick, fleshy, deeply colored root, which grows underground, and feathery green leaves that emerge above the ground. Fresho brings you the flavour and richness of the finest red, crispy and juicy carrots.

TOMATO
